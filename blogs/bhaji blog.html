Kanda Bhaji are fried onion fritters.


These crisp fritters are prepared mainly with onion and gram flour (besan).


It is a popular street food snack in Maharashtra.


There are many variations to preparing them.


Bhaji can be eaten during any time of the day like breakfast, lunch, or in the evening .
